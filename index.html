How It Works

Our advanced AI analyzes medical images (X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans) for signs of manipulation, AI generation, or doctoring. The system examines various factors including:

  • AI-powered deep learning analysis
  • Image type detection (X-ray vs MRI vs CT)
  • MRI-specific analysis (slice thickness, artifacts, contrast)
  • CT-specific analysis (Hounsfield units, beam hardening, metal artifacts)
  • Image metadata and compression artifacts
  • Statistical patterns and noise analysis
  • Anatomical consistency and medical plausibility
  • AI generation fingerprints and artifacts
